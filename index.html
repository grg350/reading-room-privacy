Privacy Policy for The Reading Room

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Effective date: March 10, 2026

The Reading Room ("we," "our," or "the app") is a personal library management application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use The Reading Room on iOS or other supported platforms.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (stored in encrypted form by our authentication provider)
  • Display name or profile information you choose to provide

1.2 Library and usage data

To provide the app's features, we store:

  • Books you add (titles, authors, descriptions, categories, cover images)
  • Borrowing records (who borrowed which book and when)
  • Your reviews and ratings
  • Preferences and in-app settings

1.3 Optional: Cover scan (Gemini)

If you use the "Scan from cover" feature, a photo of the book cover is sent to Google's Gemini API to extract title, author, and description. That image is processed according to Google's privacy practices. We do not permanently store the image after processing unless you save the book (in which case only the extracted text and any cover image you upload are stored in our systems).

1.4 Device and technical data

We may receive generic technical information (e.g. device type, OS version) to support stability and compatibility. We do not track you across other apps or websites for advertising.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the app (library, borrowings, reviews, sync)
  • Authenticate you and manage your account
  • Process cover scans when you use the scan feature
  • Store and serve book cover images and related data
  • Improve the app and fix issues
  • Comply with applicable law and enforce our terms

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each with its own privacy practices:

  • Supabase – Account sign-in and database storage. See Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase (Google) – Cloud storage for images (e.g. book covers). See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini – Used only when you choose "Scan from cover"; processes the image to extract book details. See Google Privacy Policy.

By using the app, you agree to the use of these services as described in this policy and in their respective policies.

4. Data Storage and Security

Account and library data are stored in secure, access-controlled systems (Supabase). Cover images may be stored in Firebase Storage. We use industry-standard practices to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and portability: You can view and manage your library and profile data within the app.
  • Correction: You can edit your profile and book data in the app.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us (see Contact below). We will process such requests in line with applicable law.
  • Opt-out of cover scan: Do not use the "Scan from cover" feature if you do not want images sent to Google's Gemini service.

If you are in the European Economic Area, UK, or other regions with similar laws, you may have additional rights (e.g. right to object, restrict processing). Contact us to exercise them.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and library data for as long as your account is active. After you request account deletion, we delete or anonymize your data within a reasonable period, except where we must retain it for legal or safety reasons.

7. Children's Privacy

The Reading Room is not directed at children under 13 (or the applicable age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using the app, you consent to such transfer. We ensure appropriate safeguards where required by law.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy in the app or on our website and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
